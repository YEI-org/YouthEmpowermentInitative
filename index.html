About YEI

As part of the WeAspire program, the Youth Empowerment Initiative (YEI) is dedicated to ending youth homelessness. We believe that every young person deserves to be sheltered, schooled, and empowered. YEI offers comprehensive services to help homeless youth break the cycle of homelessness and become self-reliant citizens.

Our Services

Housing & Shelter

YEI provides safe, temporary housing to homeless youth. We ensure that every individual has a warm place to stay, with the aim of transitioning them into long-term housing solutions.

Food & Essentials

Our food center offers nutritious meals, and we also provide clothing and other basic necessities to support the health and well-being of the youth.

Education & Job Training

We offer education programs and job readiness training to help youth gain the skills needed for the workforce and support them in finding employment opportunities.

Mental Health Support

YEI provides mental health services, including counseling and emotional support, to address trauma and other challenges faced by homeless youth, helping them rebuild their lives.
